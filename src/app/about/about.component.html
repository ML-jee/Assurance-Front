About us
Welcome to our decentralized microservices architecture, accessible through a dynamic SPA/WPA frontend. Our platform revolutionizes insurance contract management with smart contracts, offering transparency and cost-efficiency.

Our backend, powered by Spring Boot and DevOps tools ( Git, Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins), efficiently handles insurance business logic. Additionally, our recommendation system, driven by machine learning algorithms, tailors offers to individual profiles.

With Flask and MLOps tools (Git, Docker, AirFlow, Kafka), we deploy high-performing models, utilizing Kubeflow for streamlined pipelines. Join us in reshaping the insurance landscape with transparency, innovation, and efficiency.
